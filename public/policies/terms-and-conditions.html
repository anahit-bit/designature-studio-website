3. AI Concepts Tool

3.1 What It Does

The AI Concepts tool allows you to upload a photograph of your room and inspiration images, select a style preference, and receive AI-generated interior design concept images based on your inputs. This is a free demonstration feature intended to give visitors a sense of AI-assisted design possibilities.

3.2 Google Sign-In

To access the AI Concepts tool, you are required to sign in with your Google account. This sign-in is used solely to manage your free generation allowance (3 free generations per account) and to prevent abuse. We collect your Google account name, email address, and profile photo for this purpose only.

3.3 Free Generation Allowance

Each Google account is entitled to three (3) free AI concept generations. This allowance is tracked server-side and tied to your Google account. Once your three free generations are used, you will be invited to contact us to discuss further options.

3.4 Image Processing

Images you upload to the AI Concepts tool are sent to Google's Gemini AI model for processing. Uploaded images are used solely to generate your requested design output. They are not stored permanently on our servers, and are not used to train AI models or shared with third parties for any other purpose.

AI-generated concepts are provided for inspirational purposes only. They do not constitute professional interior design advice, architectural drawings, or construction documentation. Results may vary and are not guaranteed to be accurate, complete, or suitable for any specific purpose.
